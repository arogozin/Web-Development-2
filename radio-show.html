Electronic Family – Live Broadcast

11am – 12pm Mondays

Electronic Family descends on the Amsterdamse Bos once again on 19 July 2014, turning the fields of the forest in south of Amsterdam into an immense dance floor for one of the world’s largest outdoor trance festivals!

Enchanting trance festival
The 2014 edition of the thumping festival will once again be bursting at the seams with the main Family Stage being complemented by the Armada, Grotesque and Global Grooves stages.

With major names including Armin van Buuren, Cosmic Gate with Emma Hewitt, Andrew Rayel and KhoMha backed up by plenty of up-and-coming talent from the house, trance and dance scenes, this year’s festival looks set to be bigger, better and louder than ever!
