Stream Tomorrowland EDM Fest’s Second Weekend Live in Belgium All WeekendSeptember 31, 2014

While Ultra in Miami and Electric Daisy Carnival in Las Vegas rule the American EDM festival scene, Belgium’s massive Tomorrowland is among Europe’s biggest and most popular dance party weekenders, and the fest’s second weekend of revelry (July 25-27) is in full swing. This year’s Tomorrowland is proving to be one of the in-demand yet, with all 360,000 tickets for the event selling out in less than an hour after going on sale back in February.
